Francis Fukuyama - Traveling 'Rock Star
In mid-October, Council members had the privilege of attending our two programs featuring political scientist and author Francis Fukuyama when he discussed his book Political Order and Political Decay: From the Industrial Revolution to the Globalization of Democracy. He's continues to get attention with his work as evidenced in a weekend story in the United Kingdom's Guardian newspaper. He told an interviewer that "in recently democratized countries…I'm still a rock star." And, he added, "To be able to go to Kiev and tell people there that democracy still remains the wave of the future - it's in those moments that I feel most fully that I've made and am making a lasting contribution."

In pursuit of a human right…
The Council's 2013 International Educator of the year, Bhavani Parpia, continues to make headlines. This time Fort Worth Weekly examines what inspired this remarkable North Texas educator to take up the ambitious cause of educating children around the world. It's a story of personal involvement serving as a magnet that draws others to the challenge of helping these young students. Bhavani's mission continues to grow while embracing the theme that education is, in her words, "that most fundamental human right."
